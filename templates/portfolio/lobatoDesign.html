Lobato Poster Design

Inspired to mimic the Pablo Lobato style illustration of, Zach Galifianakis(Left) & Iron Mike Tyson (Right).

Zack is one of my favourite movie stars, and Mike Tyson is one of my favourite boxers. This scene was inspired from the movie, ‘Hangover’ (by Todd Philips), so I made a funky looking poster, making an official match between Alain vs Tyson. I believe this is very successful piece of artwork, considering the theme.
