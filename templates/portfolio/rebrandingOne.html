Marianne's Boutique Rebranding

Marianne’s boutique is a Ottawa based women’s clothing store, they cary a wide range of women’s bra’s in all shapes and sizes, and specialize in mastectomy.

The client was unable to successfully market themselves, and had a very out dated website, which was not clean nor responsive. After some research and speaking with the clients, staff and customers, I decided that the best way to solve their problem, was to redesign their website.

After redesigning the website, the new design is genuine to the brand.
