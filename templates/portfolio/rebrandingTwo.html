Chips and Dairy Rebranding

This was a re-branding assignment of a local business in Ottawa, Ontario. I chose ‘Chips and Dairy’, a Canadian/Chinese restaurant.

The challenge I faced was how to show the identity of both cultures in the logo of the restaurant. Therefore, I went with a very clean western type face, while the ampersand sign mimics a soup bowl with chopsticks. Thus representing the Asian culture.

The outcome was very successful, as it shows both the Chinese and Canadian cultures in the brand, yet very simple.
